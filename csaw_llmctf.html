This page hosts the leaderboard for the Agentic Automated CTF Competition 2025 Jointly with CSAW'25 being held from 01st July to 8th October 2025. The challenge asks participants to create an LLM agent that can solve CTF challenges. Examples of LLM agents are the baseline agent presented in the NYU CTF Bench, and the EnIGMA agent that achieves state-of-the-art results.
